About me

I am a designer from Graz, Austria. My design philosophy revolves around the user and is based on the understanding that good design involves solving problems and creating great experiences.

Before starting with user experience design i worked in a collective of designers, photographers & developers located in Graz, offering a wide range of creative services, including branding, photography, digital product design, print design, copywriting and web development.

Since 2016, I am riding the wave as a fulltime user experience designer for the Blue Tomato webshop, the epicenter of all things boardsport, optimizing the digital customer experience.
